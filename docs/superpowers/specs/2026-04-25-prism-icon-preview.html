Warm vs Cool Contrast

The Ethereal Refractor

We solved the "white-on-white" issue by using temperature contrast instead of harsh dark/light contrast. The base plate is now a warm, soft Sand/Cream color. The glass prism is cast in a cool, icy Cyan tint. This immediately creates beautiful 3D depth and hierarchy, while the flowing Coral ribbon adds the lively energy.

The 3-Color Palette:
  • 1. Warm Sand (Base): A soft, grounded off-white that prevents the icon from looking like an empty PNG background.
  • 2. Icy Cyan (Glass): Gives the prism a distinct physical volume and a beautiful cool/warm contrast against the base.
  • 3. Coral / Peach (Light): A highly energetic but soft accent color for the dynamic ribbon.

Fresh Botanical Tech

Soft Mint & Indigo Layers

We completely dropped the purple background. Purple and white can often feel dated. Instead, we moved to an extremely fresh, modern "Soft Mint/Sage" background. To ensure the delicate thin-line logo stands out beautifully on the white glass, we colored it a deep, rich Indigo. It feels airy, natural, yet highly technical.

The 3-Color Palette:
  • 1. Soft Mint / Sage (Base): A lively, breathing color that feels modern, fresh, and completely non-artificial.
  • 2. Clean White (Glass): The frosted glass layer provides a pure canvas.
  • 3. Deep Indigo (Logo): An elegant, dark contrasting color for the airy monoline strokes, ensuring perfect legibility without resorting to harsh black.
